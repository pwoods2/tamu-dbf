About Us!

The Texas A&M Design, Build, Fly (TAMU DBF) team was established in . TAMU DBF participates in the American Institute of Aeronautics and Astronautics (AIAA) DBF competition annually. In this competition, teams are given a detailed mission profile with requirements and constraints, and each team must submit an initial proposal for consideration. The top 110 proposals from across the globe advance to the next stage where they design and build their aircraft, while simultaneously creating their design reports for submission in February. The competition takes place in April, at varying locations. TAMU DBF is honored to represent Texas A&M through this international competition.

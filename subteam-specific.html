Meet Our Subteams!

TAMU DBF is composed of 4 subteams that work together year-round to ensure success at competition. We are led by an 8 person leadership team that guides and plans the year with member input, with special emphasis on teaching and growing our members. Click on each tab above to learn more about our specific subteams.

Our subteams and their jobs:

  • Stability and Control:
    Initial aircraft design, with emphasis on scoring analysis, simulations, and sizing.
  • Structures and Manufacturing:
    Aircraft modeling, manufacturing, and mission specific work, following initial design.
  • Avionics and Propulsion:
    Electronics design and manufacturing, alongside flight testing and mission work.
  • Business and Logistics:
    Technical writing and budget planning, as well as maintenance of public facing media.
